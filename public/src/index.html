What is Please Find My Animal?

Have you lost your pet? Please Find My Animal is a new way to find them. Using a combination of traditional signs along with the power of your neighbor's smartphones, you'll increase the odds that you'll be reunited with your missing pet.

Have you found a stray? Please Find My Animal will help you find its owner. No longer will you need to just drop the animal off at a shelter, always wondering if they made it home. Now you can rest easy knowing that you've made a difference.

How does it work?

As soon as you lose your pet send a Missing Pet Alert. All Please Find My Animal users within a 5 mile radius are immediately alerted. When your pet is found, Please Find My Animal alerts you, and you can arrange to meet the rescuer to recover your pet. All communications are handled within the app, so there's no need to paste your phone number on every telephone pole in sight, or to share any personal information at all.

FAQ

What is Please Find My Animal?
Please Find My Animal is a service that helps reunite people with their missing pets.
What do I do if I lost my pet?
If you've already set up a Pet Profile for your pet, then all you need to do is tap "I'm Lost", and Please Find My Animal immediatly gets to work.
What happens when I tap "I'm Lost"?
When you click I'm Lost on your Pet Profile, an alert is immediately sent to all users of Please Find My Animal who are registered within a 5 mile radius of the location you last saw your pet. You'll have the option of automatically using your home location — which you set up when you create your Personal Profile — or choosing the location on a map. That way you're always reaching out to the people who are most able to help.
Can I also let my Facebook Friends know when I lose my pet?
Please Find My Animal integrates with Facebook and Twitter, and you can choose to send a status update or tweet to let your friends know that you need their help.
